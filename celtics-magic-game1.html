Celtics Cruise to Victory Over Magic

The Boston Celtics easily handled the Orlando Magic in Game 1, winning with a score of 1-0. Jayson Tatum led the Celtics with a dominant 35 points, showcasing his scoring prowess and versatility. Paolo Banchero had 20 points for the Magic, but the Celtics' defense proved too strong.

The Celtics' defense was stifling, limiting the Magic to just 38% shooting from the field. Jaylen Brown added 24 points for the Celtics, while Marcus Smart contributed 10 points and 8 assists.
