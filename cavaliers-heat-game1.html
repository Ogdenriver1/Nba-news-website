Cavaliers Dominate Heat in Game 1

The Cleveland Cavaliers started their playoff run strong, dominating the Miami Heat in Game 1 with a final score of 1-0. Donovan Mitchell led the Cavaliers with an impressive 30 points, showcasing his offensive prowess. Bam Adebayo contributed 20 points for the Heat, but it wasn't enough to overcome the Cavaliers' strong defense and balanced scoring attack.

The Cavaliers' defense held the Heat to just 40% shooting from the field, while the Cavaliers shot 50% from the field. The Cavaliers also outrebounded the Heat 45-35.
