Warriors Steal Game 1 from Rockets

The Golden State Warriors stole Game 1 from the Houston Rockets with a score of 1-0. Stephen Curry led the Warriors with 34 points, including several clutch shots down the stretch. Jalen Green had 29 points for the Rockets, but it wasn't enough to overcome the Warriors' experience and championship pedigree.

Klay Thompson added 22 points for the Warriors, while Alperen Sengun had 20 points and 10 rebounds for the Rockets. The Warriors shot 46% from the field, while the Rockets shot 44% from the field.
