Knicks Take Game 1 Against Pistons

The New York Knicks defeated the Detroit Pistons in Game 1 of their series with a score of 1-0. Jalen Brunson led the Knicks with 28 points, showcasing his scoring ability and playmaking skills. Cade Cunningham had 22 points for the Pistons, but the Knicks' defense proved too strong.

The Knicks' strong defensive performance was key to their victory, as they limited the Pistons to just 42% shooting from the field. The Knicks also dominated the boards, outrebounding the Pistons 48-38. Julius Randle added 18 points and 12 rebounds for the Knicks.
