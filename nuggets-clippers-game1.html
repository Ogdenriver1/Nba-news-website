Nuggets Overcome Clippers in Game 1

The Denver Nuggets defeated the Los Angeles Clippers in Game 1 with a score of 1-0. Nikola Jokic led the Nuggets with 27 points and 12 rebounds, showcasing his all-around dominance. Kawhi Leonard had 26 points for the Clippers, but the Nuggets' balanced scoring attack proved too much to handle.

Jamal Murray added 20 points for the Nuggets, while Paul George had 18 points for the Clippers. The Nuggets shot 48% from the field, while the Clippers shot 45% from the field.
