Timberwolves Upset Lakers in Game 1

The Minnesota Timberwolves upset the Los Angeles Lakers in Game 1 with a score of 1-0. Anthony Edwards led the Timberwolves with a game-high 36 points, showcasing his athleticism and scoring ability. LeBron James had 28 points for the Lakers, but the Timberwolves' defense and balanced scoring proved too much to overcome.

Karl-Anthony Towns added 22 points and 10 rebounds for the Timberwolves, while Anthony Davis had 25 points and 12 rebounds for the Lakers. The Timberwolves shot 45% from the field, while the Lakers shot 42% from the field.
