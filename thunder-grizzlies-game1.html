Thunder Strike First Against Grizzlies

The Oklahoma City Thunder defeated the Memphis Grizzlies in Game 1 with a score of 1-0. Shai Gilgeous-Alexander led the Thunder with an impressive 31 points, showcasing his scoring ability and playmaking skills. Ja Morant had 25 points for the Grizzlies, but the Thunder's defense proved too strong.

The Thunder's defense was key to their victory, limiting the Grizzlies to just 41% shooting from the field. Chet Holmgren added 15 points and 10 rebounds for the Thunder, while Jaren Jackson Jr. had 20 points for the Grizzlies.
