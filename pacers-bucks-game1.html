Pacers Edge Out Bucks in Close Game 1

The Indiana Pacers secured a narrow victory over the Milwaukee Bucks, winning Game 1 with a score of 1-0. Tyrese Haliburton led the Pacers with 25 points and 10 assists, orchestrating the offense with precision. Giannis Antetokounmpo had a dominant performance with 32 points and 15 rebounds for the Bucks, but it wasn't enough to secure the win.

The Pacers' three-point shooting proved to be the difference, as they connected on 40% of their attempts from beyond the arc, compared to just 30% for the Bucks. The Pacers also had fewer turnovers, which helped them maintain possession and control the tempo of the game.
